How It Works

My Food Bag is where delicious flavour meets easy convenience. Our team of expert recipe developers are led by Nadia to create meals you love, all you need to do is cook & enjoy!

You Choose

We design our food bags to delight pretty much everyone, from fussy pre-schoolers to foodies with finesse. Select from our wide range of bags and extras. Choose your subscription frequency and have the deliciousness arrive either weekly or fortnightly.

We Create

Every week, we dream up wonderful new dinner recipes inspired by Nadia's Nude Food philosophy and the My Food Bag Kitchen. Check out our mouth-watering line-ups below!

We Source & Deliver

We receive your order by Monday midnight then source the freshest New Zealand produce, meat and fish – always free-range and sustainably sourced. Our deliciously easy recipes are delivered to your door on Saturday, Sunday or Monday depending on your area.

You Cook & Enjoy

The joy begins! Our recipes are ready in no time, with tips to unleash your inner chef. We make dinner something to look forward to, you'll wonder how you ever got by before!

Cancellation Policy

No Cancellations or Skips after 11:59pm on the Monday before the relevant Delivery Date. In order to create your food bag each week our team starts sourcing immediately after orders lock at Monday midnight. Once the ingredients for your bag have started to be sourced, we are unable to accept cancellations after this point.

Refund Policy

There are certain situations where refunds or credits may be granted, please see our policy page in our Help Centre for more information. Refunds or credits are not provided for accidental orders or change of mind. We process all refunds immediately. Any refunds or credits are subject to our T&Cs.

Address Policy

Your delivery information will be confirmed at point of order. Any redirection of your order must be made by Tuesday 7 PM in your account. Please note, redirections can only be made within your general region after 11:59pm Monday cut-off.
